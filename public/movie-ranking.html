Best Romantic Comedies According to Diane

My top 3 Favorite Romantic Comedies

When Harry Met Sally

This is a must see for everyone. Classic lines and the interaction between Meg Ryan and Billy Crystal is inspiring. In fact this chemistry is the basis for all of Nora Ephron's hits that followed.

Pretty Woman

Every girl's cinderalla dream. This timely update on the cinderalla story is an entertaining ride and provides laughter as well as tears. Exactly what you want in a romantic comedy

The Devil Wears Prada

Another classic retelling of how we can miss what is right in front of us chasing blind ambition. Must see for every 20 something woman to keep her grounded!
